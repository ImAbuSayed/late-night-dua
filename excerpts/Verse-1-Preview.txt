Shehar di iss motti bhid vich, walking alone
Main taan dil ton tainu labhda haan, into the unknown
Teri hassi di awaaz sunda, in my head
Main taan jaagda hi rehnda haan, inside my bed
Kaale rang da tera libaas, looking so rare
Teri zulfan di sohni khushboo, fills the air
Main taan mud-mud vekhda haan, catching your gaze
Tere kheyalan vich gumm haan, lost in the maze
